Our Story

Founded in 2020, Dudat Sales & Marketing was created by Duda Marthaus.

Born in Brazil and later moving to Dallas, Texas, Duda graduated from Southern Methodist University (SMU), where she combined her passion for business, marketing, and leadership.

About five years ago, Duda began two journeys that shaped her career: sales and social media.

On one side, she built and led a sales team of 20+ women, training them and driving measurable results. That experience built her discipline, leadership skills, and deep understanding of how to connect with people and drive growth.

At the same time, she was growing as a content creator on TikTok, reaching 400K followers and over 11 million likes in just 3 months. This proved one thing: authentic content, when done strategically, performs.

She realized businesses needed that same approach, content that feels real, relatable, and engaging, not just promotional.

During her sophomore year of college, she launched Dudat Sales & Marketing, combining sales expertise with high-performing social strategy.

Today, Duda has built a team and leads with passion, precision, and perfectionism. Having traveled to 40+ countries, her marketing perspective blends international trends with local strategy, giving clients a competitive edge.

Dudat Sales & Marketing helps businesses grow through:

Social media management Content creation Website design SEO Google & Meta Ads Branding AI messaging systems

Always with one goal: create content that feels human and drives real results.
