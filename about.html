Our Story

Founded in 2020, Dudat Sales & Marketing was created by Duda Marthaus. Born in Brazil and later moving to Dallas, Texas, Duda graduated from SMU, combining her passion for business, marketing, and leadership.

She built and led a sales team of 20+ women, driving measurable results. At the same time, she grew as a content creator on TikTok — reaching 400K followers and over 11 million likes in just 3 months.

She realized businesses needed that same approach: content that feels real, relatable, and engaging. Today, Duda leads a team with passion, precision, and perfectionism to drive real results.
